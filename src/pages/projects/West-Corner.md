## Neighbors
Neighbors is a social hangout game on **ROBLOX** owned by **West Corner**. It is inspired by **Omeagle**.

## The Corner
The Corner, and it's subplaces, surround a group of games which share data between each other. It maintains a profile system, with reputation and comments you can add onto people's profiles.
